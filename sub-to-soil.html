Questions

Asked often.

What exactly is spent substrate?

It's the block of hardwood sawdust and grain our mushrooms grow from. After a harvest, the block is still packed with nitrogen, phosphorus, and living mycelium — too valuable to throw away, and exactly what tired garden soil is missing.

Is the consultation really free?

Completely. We visit, assess light, drainage, and space, and leave you a rough plan and honest pricing. If it isn't a fit, you keep the advice.

How much does a garden cost?

It depends on size and scope — a single raised bed is a very different project from a full backyard plot. Every design is priced transparently after your consultation, with options for each budget.

Do you maintain the garden too?

We set you up to succeed on your own — with planting maps, a seasonal calendar, and our growers a phone call away. Ongoing maintenance visits are available if you'd like the help.

Where do you build gardens?

We serve families across New Jersey, with priority around our Fairfield farm. If you're near the Tri-State edges, ask — we say yes more often than not.
