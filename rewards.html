The Mycelium Club

Loyalty, cultivated.

Everything you do with the farm feeds your account — purchases, reviews, shares, referrals, even your birthday. Points become farm credit. Simple as that.

Earning Spores

Six ways to grow your balance.

Purchases · 1 pt / $1

Every dollar spent on fresh mushrooms, wellness, boxes, or subscriptions earns a point — subscriptions earn faster the longer they run.

Farm pickup · 2× points

Skip delivery and grab your order in Fairfield — every pickup order earns double points on the whole purchase. Cheaper for you, kinder to the planet, better for us.

Reviews · 100 pts

Share an honest review of something you actually ordered — good, bad, or in between. Honest words move us forward.

Social sharing · 50 pts

Tag the farm in a post or story showing your harvest or ritual. One credit per platform, per month.

Referrals · 250 pts

The biggest earner: when a friend you referred places their first order, a quarter-thousand points land in your account. Refer a friend.

Birthdays · 100 pts

Tell us your birthday when you join and the farm remembers it every year — automatically.

Redeeming

Points convert to farm credit at checkout — starting at 250 points. Exact redemption tiers announced at launch.
