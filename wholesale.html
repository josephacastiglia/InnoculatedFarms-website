Onboarding

Three steps. One conversation.

Most wholesale relationships start with a credit application and a six-week wait. Ours starts with a phone call and a box of mushrooms.

01

One conversation

Fifteen minutes on the phone or a farm visit — tell us your menu, your volumes, and your delivery days.

02

A sample box, on us

We send your kitchen a curated sample of every variety you're considering. Cook with it. Judge us on it.

03

Your standing order

Locked pricing, a weekly cut-to-order schedule, and one text message to adjust quantities. That's the whole system.

The Fine Print, Unfined

What you get, plainly.

Cut to order

Nothing sits in cold storage waiting for a truck. We harvest against your order the morning it goes out — that's the whole reason the shelf life is different.

Tri-state delivery & pickup

Our own vehicles across the tri-state area on a set weekly route, or pick up at the Fairfield farm on your schedule.

Volume pricing that scales

Pricing steps down as your standing order grows, locked for the term so you can cost a menu without guessing.

A grower on the other end
